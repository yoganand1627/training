SUMMARY OF APS FINDINGS AND ACTIONS:

[[txtSummary]]

ACTION REQUESTED OF PROBATE COURT

Please notify the APS contact person specified above if further information or service coordination is needed. If you determine that this is not a guardianship within your jurisdiction, please telephone the APS contact person.

Please note that, by law (Section 48.101, Human Resources Code) the name of the complainant (i.e., the reporter or informant) is confidiental. Consequently, identifying information about the complainant is not included in this report. If you need this information in your review of the guardianship, please telephone the contact person specified above. The APS worker or supervisor may orally share information about the complainant’s identity with the court.
