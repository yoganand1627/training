[[EMAB_ALLEGATION]] :  Mental or emotional injury to a child that results in an observable and material impairment in the child's growth, development, or psychological functioning; or

[[MDNG_ALLEGATION]] :  The failure to seek, obtain, or follow through with medical care for the child, with the failure resulting in or presenting a substantial risk of death, disfigurement, or bodily injury or with the failure resulting in an observable and material impairment to the growth, development, or functioning of the child.
 
[[NSUP_ALLEGATION]] :  Placing the child in or failure to remove the child from a situation that a reasonable person would realize requires judgment or actions beyond the child's level of maturity, physical condition, or mental abilities and that results in bodily injury or a substantial risk of immediate harm to the child; or

[[PHAB_ALLEGATION]] :  Physical injury that results in substantial harm to the child, or the genuine threat of substantial harm from physical injury to the child, including an injury that is at variance with the history or explanation given and excluding an accident or reasonable discipline by a parent, guardian, or managing or possessory conservator that does not expose the child to a substantial risk of harm; or
