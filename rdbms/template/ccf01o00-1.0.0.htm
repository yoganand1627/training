RE: HB 1356 - Tuition Waiver

Dear [[YOUTH_FIRST_NAME2]] [[YOUTH_MIDDLE_NAME2]] [[YOUTH_LAST_NAME2]] [[YOUTH_NAME_SUFFIX2]],

This is to inform you that Chapter 54 of the Texas Education Code entitles you to attend state supported universities, colleges, and vocational schools with the tuition and all fees waived except for Building Use Fee (sometimes called General Use Fee). This waiver does not apply to dormitory, lodging or food expenses, for which you are responsible. In order to facilitate your enrollment at these institutions, this letter serves to confirm that you were in substitute care and under the conservatorship of the Department of Protective and Regulatory Services on or after the day preceding your 18th birthday. In order to take advantage of tuition and fee waiver, you must enroll in school within three (3) years of your discharge from care. Your discharge date was [[DISCHARGE_DATE]].

I encourage you to contact the institution's Registrar's Office prior to registration to determine if any further documentation and/or action is necessary to ensure you will receive the waiver.
