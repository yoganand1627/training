Payment Of Care Help

 
Court Review Due By:

Unless otherwise specified by the judge, a court review for a custodian who is an in-state relative is due within 36 months of the transfer of custody and every 36 months thereafter, until the child turns 18; for a custodian who is an out-of-state relative, the court review is due within 12 months of the transfer of custody and every 12 months thereafter, until the child turns 18.

Whether or not the court has designated DFCS as the entity responsible for the court report, the SSCM assigned to an RCS/ERCS case should assist in monitoring that consistent periodic reviews are occurring.

Court Reviews are not required for Relatives or Non-Relatives who have been granted Guardianship.
