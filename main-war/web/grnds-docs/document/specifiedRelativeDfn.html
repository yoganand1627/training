Specified Relative


The following relationship meet the requirements of the specified relative:

  • parents (either by birth, legal adoption or step-relationship)
  • grandparents (up to great-great-great)
  • siblings (whole, half or step)
  • aunts/uncles (up to great-great)
  • nieces/nephews (up to great-great)
  • first cousin
  • first cousin once removed (the child of a first cousin)
  • spouses of any person named in the above group, even after the marriage is terminated by death or divorce.
EXCEPTION: The spouse of a stepparent or the spouse of a stepsibling is NOT within the specified degree of relationship.
